About GraspIQ

GraspIQ presents a revolutionary educational platform that integrates AI and biometric technology to personalize the learning experience. By analysing real-time physiological and behavioural data, it adapts educational content to suit individual cognitive and emotional states.

We are on a mission to make learning more accessible, engaging, and attuned to the individual needs of students worldwide, preparing them for a successful future in an ever-evolving world.
